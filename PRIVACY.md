# Privacy Policy

FastTimes is an open-source application built with a privacy-first approach. This policy explains how your data is handled.

## Data Collection & Storage

**Your data stays on your device.**

- **Local Storage:** All fasting data, history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using an encrypted-ready Room database and Jetpack DataStore.
- **No Cloud Sync:** FastTimes does not have a backend server. We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to any external servers or cloud services.
- **No Personal Information:** We do not require you to create an account or provide any personally identifiable information (PII) to use the app.

## Third-Party Services

- **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics (like Firebase Analytics or Mixpanel) to track your behavior.
- **No Advertisements:** FastTimes is ad-free and does not include any advertising SDKs that could track you.
- **No Tracking:** There are no trackers or "phone home" mechanisms in the code.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions only to provide core functionality:
- **Notifications:** Used solely to alert you when your fast starts or ends.

## Open Source

As an open-source project licensed under **GNU GPL v3.0**, you are welcome to audit the source code yourself to verify our privacy claims.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the `PRIVACY.md` file in the official repository.
