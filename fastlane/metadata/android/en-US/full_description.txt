FastTimes is a simple, lightweight, and completely open-source intermittent fasting tracker designed with your privacy in mind.

Features:
<ul>
<li><b>Material 3 Expressive Design</b>: Experience a modern, bold, and intuitive interface built with the latest Material 3 Expressive design language.</li>
<li><b>Privacy First</b>: No accounts, no cloud sync, and no trackers. All your data stays securely on your device.</li>
<li><b>Detailed Fasting History</b>: Keep a comprehensive log of every fast. Review, edit, and manage your past entries with ease.</li>
<li><b>Performance Statistics</b>: Visualize your progress with in-depth statistics. Track your streaks, total fasting time, and trends to stay motivated.</li>
<li><b>Material You Customization</b>: Personalize your experience with dynamic color support that matches your wallpaper and flexible fasting profiles tailored to your lifestyle.</li>
<li><b>Local Notifications</b>: Stay on track with timely alerts when your fast begins or ends, handled entirely on-device.</li>
<li><b>Open Source</b>: Licensed under GNU GPL v3.0, ensuring transparency and community-driven development.</li>
</ul>

Whether you're just starting your intermittent fasting journey or are a seasoned pro, Fast Times provides powerful, professional tools to track your health without the bloat or the surveillance.
